About

I am a Lecturer (Assistant Professor equivalent) at Monash University. My research aims at building Trustworthy AI, with a focus on robustness and interpretability of neural models, as well as their downstream applications in Natural Language Processing (NLP). I have led teams in multiple NLP competitions, earning top honors. My research has been published in several top-tier venues in AI and NLP, including EMNLP, ICLR, and AAAI. I regularly serves as a reviewer for international conferences in my field.
